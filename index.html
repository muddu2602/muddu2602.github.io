About Us

The JNTUK University College of Engineering Vizianagaram was established in the academic year 2007-08 with five Under Graduate Engineering Courses Viz., B.Tech in EEE, ECE, CSE, ME, IT, CIVIL and METALLURGY. Now it is a well established constituent college under JNTU Kakinada, and has a very fast-developing infrastructure.

ITYUKTA 2K18 is a national level student technical symposium. IT means Information Technology and YUKTA means to group; ITYUKTA means a platform where the information regarding different technologies is grouped together.

Technical Events

  • Blind- Coding
  • Web Design
  • Puzzle The Code
  • Detect The Diagram
  • Paper Presentation

“Blind coding” sounds funny right!!! Yeah!! But it’s quite interesting.You might have attended many coding contests but this time we are here with a distinct notion.The main motive of this is that the participant has to type the code when the screen is off.After that the code will be compiled.The one who successfully executes the code will be the ultimate winner.

Are you looking for a perfect platform where you can present your attractive and creative web pages?? Then this would be the best opportunity you can grab! In this event,you have to design your own webpages based on a given theme beforehand and present them in our technical fest.The most attractive and the best webpage will be awarded.(THEME WILL BE ANNOUNCED SOON).

Puzzling is fun and coding is interesting.A crazy combination of these two makes it much more interesting.Here is an event “puzzle the code” where the code is jumbled or puzzled and you need to rearrange the code which gives the expected output.Whoever does this job quickly will be the winner.

We all are interested in playing the detective games.Why not play with technology?here is the game for you!!You will be shown few pictures of the latest technology and innovation.All you need to do is just detect those pictures and give a brief description of that picture.That person who detects more number of pictures will be awarded.

Paper presentation is an event in which the participant presents and explains a particular topic infornt of the concerned people by means of power point presentation.Present any technical topic in a professional way.The event focuses on the topic chosen and the way it is being presented.

Non—Technical Events

  • Crypt Your Mind
  • QUIZ
  • Treasure Hunt
  • LAn Gaming
  • Captcha Cracking

“crypt your mind”...huh?Are you in a thought that it might be strenuous to perform??then you are mistaken.All you have to do is bring up your knowledge and skills together and detect the correct word.One who cracks more number of words will be the winner.Crypt your mind is a written test,where the text will be in encrypted format. Based on the key/hint the CIPHER TEXT must be decrypted so as to get the plain text. RULES.

Quiz...hmm..As you all know knowledge is just like an ocean which has no limit.Here, we come up with an event to examine your general knowledge. So,just try this out and bring out the best in you and expel out your general knowledge skills.

A treasure is something you crave for but it is not that easy to find .So,here’s the challenge for you.Try to find out our treasure with the help of clues and finally the treasure will be yours.Each team consists of a maximum of 3 members.So get ready for the challenge!!

Craze for gaming is never ending.Everyone loves playing LAN games.Here we establish a LAN connection and the devices will be connected for the purpose of playing games.The one who wins in every level will be the final winner.So,compete with your friends and have fun playing games.

“ARE YOU A ROBOT? NO,RIGHT!!” this statement is quite often seen!! So,here’s an event to make sure you are not robots!! The participants will be given a set of captchas which need to be cracked.The one who does this quickly will be choosen as the winner.
